About REEVES Research Investment Limited

Reeves Research Investment Limited is a Papua New Guinea registered research and innovation company established to conduct research, develop innovative ideas, and acquire valuable knowledge aimed at addressing environmental and industrial challenges.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to conduct research that contributes to environmental protection, scientific advancement, and sustainable development.

Areas of Interest

  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Hazardous Waste Management
  • Climate and Environmental Impacts
  • Scientific Innovation
  • Industrial Environmental Responsibility
  • Future Technologies
  • Public Health and Environmental Interactions

Research Philosophy

We believe scientific research and innovation can provide valuable insights and practical solutions to environmental challenges and support a safer, healthier, and more sustainable future.
